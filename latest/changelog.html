False Infinity Rewritten Changelog

0.1.2

  • Added this changelog.
  • Added a button that buy max matter autobuyer intervals, which is the same function as pressing the M key, for mobile devices.
  • Added overflow upgrade 8, which unlocks the 2nd matter autobuyer
  • Added stars, which increase the amount of helium generated during fusion, but make the fusion penalty worse.
  • Change the unit of energy from J to eV.
  • Added 0th(0-index) helium upgrade. When it's bought, deflation no longer resets anything.
  • Added 1st(0-index) helium upgrade that divides matter autobuyer intervals
  • Revamped energy, which is gained when you overflow during fusion, and powers.
  • Added 4 more tier 2 autobuyers.
  • Fixed matter dividing when overflowing during fusion.
  • Added extend overflow, which extends the overflow limit from 2^31-1 to 2^(n+31)-1, and lets you gain more overflow points, and lets you gain more energy during fusion.
